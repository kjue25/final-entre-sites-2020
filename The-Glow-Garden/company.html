About Us

We are a team of hardworking 7th graders who love the peaceful look of small plants. We love our Glow Garden succulents because they are a unique way to have a chill plant with nice lights to give a room the vibe it needs. Our team loves art and believe there are so many to express it, which is one reason we chose to make succulents. We found that there was a way to express ourselves while making a plant that can add peace and joy into others lives. Our team values collaboration, creativity, and kindness, which is why we make sure every single succulent is made with each of these until they reach your doorstep. We are the Glow Garden and our succulents will light up your world!

Our Story

We started as a 7th grade group of entrepreneurs in school wanting to make something fun and helpful in our entre class. We brainstormed ideas and came up with succulents, we thought it was a way to bring something peaceful into people's lives while still making them fun and meaningful to us. But we wanted to make our succulents different from other companies. We thought of our teams unique skills and came up with an idea, “We should light the succulents up!” We worked hard as a team to create our products design. But it didn't just magically appear overnight. We all had to work hard putting our unique skills into each succulent to create what you see today. After multiple tries we finally found our Glow Garden Succulents.
