Our Story


Hi there! We’re entre team 21! We’re your fellow classmates Lucy, Maureen, and Vivian! Maureen has been obsessed with making our product (tassels) ever since she saw cute tassels appearing everywhere, backpacks, necklaces, earrings, even wall hangings! Jumping on to this new trend, she decided that she needed to go whole hog, and dive into the world of tassels. When Vivian saw what Maureen was creating, she just knew that this had to be their Entre product. The tassels were stylish, small, and simple, just what you want in a product. Lucy quickly became a master of cranking out the creative product, and Tassa was born. Today, Lucy, Maureen, and Vivian want you, yes you! To join their team. With your help, we’ll be able to expand and become even more creative!
