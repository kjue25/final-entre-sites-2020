Welcome to Pack-A-Mask!

Every time you take off your mask, or bring it around with you, you bring unnecessary risks to your health and mask. Instead of wasting the precious filters(N95 or otherwise), which are expensive and hard to come by, you can preserve the lifespan of your mask’s filter. Pack-A-Mask is a double-layered
bag of fabric, that is an additional layer of protection, to keep away germs
and bacteria from your mask. This washable and reusable mask bag has a button
to keep your mask safe, all the while keeping a slight pocket of air at the
top. This prevents germs and bacteria from growing within a humid and damp environment. Finally, this bag also has a metal clasp to attach to your jeans,
or bag for easy portability. You won’t have to worry about looks either. This
bag has patterned fabric, and bright colors, that will make sure you won’t
lose the bag, and will catch the attention of others.

Our Product:

Our product Pack-A-Mask comes in 7 different designs. Lemon, Rainbow, Dark Flowers, Watermelon,
Berries, Pastel Flowers, and Polka Dots. Each pattern comes with a button that says homemade, and has
a corresponding hairtie as the clasp. It has been double layered, so there is still air flow, but keeps
germs out. Our bag is selling at $10 for 2 random bags. Additionally, you can pay $5 more to get
