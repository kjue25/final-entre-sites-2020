About Us

Our Name

We came up with Malie while planning our prototype for the first round of entre. Anna suggested that we make heating pillows because it would be nice to have something to relieve stress during COVID 19. We chose the name Malie because it is the Hawaiian word for calm, and that is what we want our customers to feel when using our pillows. We then proceeded to work on our first website for our pillows. After the prototype fair, some feedback we got was a bit frustrating because we had clearly put the information on our google site, but it still gave us some insight on what to do in the future. We now have more short and to the point paragraphs about our product so customers don’t have to read long sentences and learn more about our product. Feedbackers also gave a range of 1-10 dollars of how much they wanted to spend to get it embroidered. We decided that if we were to embroider, we would charge a higher price.
