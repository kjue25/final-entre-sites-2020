What does our heating pillows do?

At Malie, the idea of this warming pillow is to soothe and relax. Our product is microwave safe and takes only 10-20 seconds to warm up. It can also be used to be refrigerated. Our product comes lavender scented or no scent.

  • Meditation Pillow: Relax and place our heating pillow anywhere on your body after a long day of work. This is a perfect gift for people who love to do yoga or any form of activity.

  • Eye Soother: Use our product to reduce the tension or unclog a congested area. Use our product like a weighted blanket.

  • Pain Relief: If there is a place hurting, heat up this pillow. Our product is a great choice to help sore areas of your body, as well as cramps.

  • Our product can be put in the freezer as well! The pillow is not only for people who like warmth, but those who enjoy the cool. If you like to relax and sleep cooler, use your pillow this way!
