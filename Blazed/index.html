Our Candles

Our homemade candles come in 2 different scents (Peppermint and Lavendar). We make a conscious effort to make our candles have a natural feel, to make you feel calm and happy. These candles come in an 8 ounce gold tin. We originally had our candles come in brighter mandala holders, but we think that these holders will make you feel calmer.

Our Candle Holders

Our homemade candle holders are made with natural materials such as pebbles and shells. Our pebble candle holders are made in a way where the lights can shine throught the little holes between the rocks. Our shell candle holders are made in a way where the light shines from the top creating a glowing effect. The bottom of our candle holders are made from a jar lid wrapped in twine. Our candle holders also come with one free additional 0.5 ounce candle to put inside (one rose 0.5 ounce candle + one pebble candle holder OR one jasmine 0.5 ounce candle + one shell candle holder)!
