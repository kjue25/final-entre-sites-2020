Our Candle Holders

Our homemade candle holders are made with natural materials of pebbles and shells. Our pebble candle holders are made with little holes between the rocks to let out the light of the candle. The shell candle holder is made in a way where the light is cupped in which makes it shine from the top in a beautiful way. The bottom of our candle holders made from a jar lid wrapped in twine.
