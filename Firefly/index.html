Our Candles

Welcome to Firefly, a 100% vegan candle company. Our candles are here to help you calm down in stressful times. They come in various scents including rosemary-lavender, peppermint and lavender. The scents are mild so that they aren't too overwhelming. Our candles come in small jars that measure 2.2 inches tall, and 2.5 inches wide.

Why soy wax candles?

Soy wax candles are good for the environment and you. Unlike paraffin wax, soy wax is a renewable resource. Paraffin wax is made from petroleum which has to be mined and causes habitat destruction. Also unlike beeswax soy wax is vegan. Bees need beeswax for making honey and caring for their young. Without beeswax bees immune system is weakened making them more susceptible to disease and death. Soy wax candles also have a longer burn time than paraffin candles and are frangreced with natural essential oils.

Here at Firefly we strive to bring peace to the homes of our customers.
