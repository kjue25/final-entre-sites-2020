Costs of goods

Our cost of goods per sticker is 27 cents. According to our feedback forms a little more than a half of our customers are willing to pay one dollar or more (58 out of 103) and 37 percent are willing to pay 50 cents (38 out of 103 ) With these prices we could have a profitable business. Only 1.9 percent of people filling out the form are uninterested in buying our product (2 out of 103). After this positive feedback we decided to keep on working on this project improving our product designs. We have a very organized work process and every member of the team has something that they can bring to the table.
