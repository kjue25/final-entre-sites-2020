About Our Company!

Our company, Everything Succulents is made up of Avery (VP of operations), Cara, (VP of operations), Leah, (VP information) Kaenna, (VP of communications), and Sophia, (VP of marketing). We make macrame terrariums and candle succulents! Our company launch was inspired by our Entrepreneurial Class at our school. We wanted our product to be something that could brighten up your day, and bring a scence of the great out doors to you, even amidst COVID-19. Succulents and candles checked all the boxes being cute, artsy, and inspired by nature. We hope you will take a look into our company and products.

Our Products!
Our products here at Everything Succulents, we bring the great outdoors straight to you with macrame terrariums and candle succulents! Our terrariums come in a glass bowl with white rocks and a faux plant, so you don't have to remember to water them! Finally, a macrame cord is tied around, so you can hang your plant! If you want, you can remove the cord, and have the plant sit on your desk, night stand or where ever you want! Our succculent candles are made with beeswax, and come in a glass jar! They will have a naturey scent, to fill your home with the sweet scent of the great outdoors. They'll look great anywhere!
