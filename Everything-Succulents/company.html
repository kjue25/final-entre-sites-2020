About Our Company!

Our company, Everything Succulents is made up of Avery (VP of operations), Cara, (VP of operations), Leah, (VP information) Kaenna, (VP of communications), and Sophia, (VP of marketing). We make macrame terrariums and candle succulents! Our company launch was inspired by our Entrepreneurial Class at our school. We wanted our product to be something that could brighten up your day, amidst COVID-19. Succulents checked all the boxes being cute, artsy, and inspired by nature. We hope you will take a look into our company and products.
